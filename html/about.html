Who we are

We are Third Harvest, an organization that fights against food waste.

Why do we care?

We care because this is what is happening on our planet â€“ One third of the food we produce go to waste.
That is roughly 1.3 billion tons.
Even if just one fourth of the food currently wasted globally could be saved, it would be enough to feed 870 million hungry people in the world.

The food we waste is enough to feed all of the world's hungry people (800 million people) four times.

What can we do?

We can do so many things...

  1. Buy only what can you definitely eat
  2. Don't throw away food (There's always another way to use it)
  3. Eat foods that are over the Best Before dates.
    Many people think that they shouldn't, but the dates are only dates that the companies recommend you to eat before.
  4. Eat the food you have!
  5. Donate to charity
  6. Eat leftovers

What are we trying to do?

Although telling families to reduce waste helps decrease the amount of food lost globally, in order to make huge changes, we need laws and rules to control companies, shops, and farmers from wasting too much food.

Third Harvest is trying to bring new laws into our country to help regulate food wsate.
